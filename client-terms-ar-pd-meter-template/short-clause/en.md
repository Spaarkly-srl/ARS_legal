## AR PD Meter (pupillary distance estimate)

Our website {{website_url}} offers **AR PD Meter**, a feature of Spaarkly s.r.l. that provides a **digital estimate of the pupillary distance** via the camera. The measurement requires **express consent given to Spaarkly** — the data controller — before it starts: the camera images are not transmitted or stored; only derived technical data, not capable on their own of identifying the User, are transmitted to Spaarkly's backend in the European Union, used to calculate the measurement and deleted immediately after processing. The [ARShades Privacy Policy](https://spaarkly-srl.github.io/ARS_legal/privacy-policy/en/) applies.

The estimate is a **preliminary digital estimate**: it is not a medical device, has no medical, clinical or diagnostic purpose and does not replace a measurement carried out by a qualified vision professional; it is reserved for users who are at least 18 years old; users under 18 may use it only with the consent and under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian.{{#prescription_sales_enabled}} For the supply of prescription lenses the estimate is never used as the definitive measurement: the pupillary distance is verified or confirmed by a qualified professional in accordance with our sales process.{{/prescription_sales_enabled}}{{^prescription_sales_enabled}} We do not sell prescription lenses through the website: the estimate is used only to support the selection of frames and non-prescription eyewear.{{/prescription_sales_enabled}}
